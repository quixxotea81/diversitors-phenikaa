The Lament

Reverberation of chaos.
A scar carved onto land.
All is stripped away then reformed anew.

The Wutheron

Discovered years following the Lament.
Able to form new substances and a conductor within reactions.

Tacet Field

A derivation of Lament, one of many breeding grounds of novelties.
A field of torment to any commoner.

The Threnodians

A dominant type of Tacet Discord formed through evolution.
Their powers outmatches the grandest of armies.

The Tacetite

Rich in value but also filled with risks.
An irreplaceable resource during the age of resurrection.

Tacet Discords

Creatures resonated from substances within Tacet Fields.
Mindless and unknown to fatigue, preying on any noise they bear.

Resonators

Genetically mutated humans triggered by Wutherons.
Each Resonator bears their own unique "frequency".

Where the story begins.
